    Helpy - Free Neighborhood Help Network

    Helpy is a free community platform where neighbors help neighbors. Post requests for transportation, groceries, childcare, tech help, manual work, medical assistance, and more. Volunteers in your area see your request and offer to help.

    Students can earn and track volunteer hours through Helpy. After helping a neighbor, log your hours directly on the platform. The person you helped verifies the hours, and they're saved permanently to your profile — perfect for high school and college community service requirements.

    Features include: real-time help requests, map view of nearby requests, direct messaging between poster and volunteer, star ratings, volunteer hour tracking, and more.

    Free to use. No ads. Built for communities.

    ✦ About Helpy

    Helpy is a neighborhood help network built at FCHacks 2026. It connects people who need small acts of help with neighbors who are willing to step up.

    Whether it's a ride to the pharmacy, help moving furniture, or someone to grab groceries — Helpy makes it easy to ask and easy to help.

    🎓 Volunteer Hours
    Students can log volunteer hours directly through Helpy after helping a neighbor. Hours are verified by the person you helped and saved to your profile.

    Cookie Policy

    Last updated: March 2026

    1. What Are Cookies

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Helpy uses browser localStorage (similar to cookies) to store settings and preferences locally on your device.

    2. What We Store Locally

    • nhn_client_id — A random ID to identify guest users so they can manage their own posts.
    • nhn_last_post_at — Timestamp of your last post, used to enforce rate limiting.
    • nhn_seen_count — Number of posts seen, used for the notification badge.
    • nhn_landing_seen — Whether you've already seen the landing page.
    • nhn_cookie_consent — Your cookie consent decision.
    • nhn_theme — Your light/dark mode preference.
    • nhn_pwa_dismissed — Whether you dismissed the app install banner.
    • msg_read_[id] — Timestamps for marking messages as read.

    3. Third-Party Services

    Helpy uses the following third-party services which may set their own cookies or store data:

    • Firebase (Google) — For authentication and real-time database. See Google's Privacy Policy.
    • Google Fonts — For typography. May log requests to Google servers.
    • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — For map tiles and geocoding.
    • Leaflet.js — Open-source mapping library, no external tracking.

    4. No Advertising Cookies

    Helpy does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or sell any user data to third parties.

    5. Managing Storage

    You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data).
